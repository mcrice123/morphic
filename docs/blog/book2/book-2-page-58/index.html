Book 2, Page 58

August 15, 2020 Maria Rice

2 minute read

Oh, I guess Errie is ok now…

Also, this page fulfills the long-lived desire of mine to tie up some loose ends. If you don’t know what I mean, maybe try re-reading Book 2. :D

More Social Media

I’ve been thinking a lot lately about next steps for Morphic and I came to the conclusion that it’s time I actually promote the webcomic more actively on social media. I’m waiting to add a link to Twitter, but here’s a link to my Instagram profile! I’ve had the account for probably a year now, but I made my first post today.

Sigh… I hope to get better at this social media thing. It’s my best bet for expanding my audience right now.

Speaking of expanding my audience…Please share!

The more people who know about my comic, the more people who will know to listen for my future crowdfund campaign for Morphic… And the more people who know about the future crowdfund campaign for Morphic, the more people who will actually pledge money on the campaign, therefore increasing the chances of Morphic meeting its goal and being printed!

And if you haven’t already, please like the “Morphic” Facebook page and subscribe to my mailing list! I appreciate all the support.

Thanks for reading and have a great weekend! I’ll be back in two weeks with the next page.
