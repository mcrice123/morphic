A Small update

Before last week, I was producing two colored pages per week for Book 2, with the intent of reaching a buffer of 8 pages. Since last week, I decided to slow down to producing one page per week, settling for a buffer of 5 pages.

…Or, rather 3 pages currently. I’m still working on the two latest pages.
