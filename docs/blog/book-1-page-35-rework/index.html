Book 1 Page 35 REWORK

March 30, 2019 Maria Rice

1 minute read

I’m satisfied with this page.

Awesome colors! I’m really happy with how well the backgrounds in the different panels complement each other.

Like a lot of pages in this scene, the rework on the inks involved fixing how I drew Capri. Although I deliberately made him a dislikable character, it’s bad when multiple panels of the character makes me grimace. I think I should have practiced drawing him a lot more before he appeared in the comic.

Fortunately, I only had to fix one panel on this page! (Capri’s curls looked a little too perfect to take him seriously…)

Anyway, here’s the link to the fully-colored page 35!

Thanks for reading!

Next week I’ll share another reworked page, as usual! Have a great weekend!
