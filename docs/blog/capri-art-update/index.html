Intermission Art #3

March 3, 2018 Maria Rice

1 minute read

The main antagonist of Book 1. I’ve been looking forward to drawing a portrait of Capri and his vines for some time.

Extending Break By Two Weeks

I’m still in the process of rewriting the series. I got really busy the past couple of weeks and I had enough time for intermission art and launching a website (and cleaning up the bugs following launch).

Not to worry though! That’s an excuse enough for me to draw more character portraits anyway. These portraits are a lot easier than the comic pages, since the focus is on viewing the character(s) from one angle instead of many. Plus, no speech bubbles.

Thanks for following and come back next week to see who is featured in the next character portrait!
